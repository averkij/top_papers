Web Agents with World Models: Learning and Leveraging Environment Dynamics in Web Navigation

1. 这篇文章讨论了大型语言模型（LLMs）在构建自主代理方面的应用。

2. 然而，当前基于LLMs的网络代理在长时间任务中表现不佳，常常出现错误，如重复购买不可退款的机票。

3. 相比之下，人类可以避免这种不可逆的错误，因为我们具有对行动可能结果的认知，即“世界模型”。

4. 受此启发，研究首先进行初步分析，确认当前LLMs（如GPT-4o，Claude-3.5-Sonnet等）缺乏世界模型。

5. 然后，提出了一种增强型世界模型（WMA）网络代理，模拟行动结果以做出更好决策。

6. 为克服训练LLMs作为世界模型的挑战，提出了一种过渡聚焦的观察抽象方法，预测目标为自然语言描述，突出时间步之间的重要状态差异。

7. 实验表明，增强型世界模型提高了代理的策略选择，并展示了代理在成本和时间效率方面的优势。

1. This article discusses the application of large language models (LLMs) in building autonomous agents.

2. However, current web agents based on LLMs perform poorly in long-term tasks and often make mistakes, such as repeatedly purchasing non-refundable airline tickets.

3. In contrast, humans can avoid such irreversible errors because we have a cognitive understanding of the possible outcomes of our actions, known as a "world model.

4. " Inspired by this, the research first conducts a preliminary analysis to confirm that current LLMs (such as GPT-4o, Claude-3.

5. 5-Sonnet, etc.

6. ) lack a world model.

7. Then, it proposes an enhanced world model (WMA) web agent that simulates the results of actions to make better decisions.

8. To overcome the challenges of training LLMs as a world model, a transition-focused observation abstraction method is proposed, which predicts targets described in natural language and highlights important state differences between time steps.

9. Experiments show that the enhanced world model improves the agent's strategy selection and demonstrates advantages in terms of cost and time efficiency.
