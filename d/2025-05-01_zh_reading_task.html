1. 这篇文章介绍了互动生成视频（IGV）技术。 Zhè piān wénzhāng jièshào le hùdòng shēngchéng shìpín (IGV) jìshù

2. IGV结合生成和互动功能，产生高质量视频内容，让用户通过控制信号和响应反馈参与其中。 IGV jiéhé shēngchéng hé hùdòng gōngnéng, chǎnshēng gāo zhìliàng shìpín nèiróng, ràng yònghù tōngguò kòngzhì xìnhào hé xiǎngyìng fǎnkuì cānyù qízhōng

3. 文章调查了IGV在游戏、人工智能和自动驾驶中的应用。 Wénzhāng diàochá le IGV zài yóuxì, rén gōng zhìnéng hé zìdòng jiàshǐ zhōng de yìngyòng

4. 还提出了理想IGV系统的五个模块：生成、控制、记忆、动态和智能。 Hái tíchū le lǐxiǎng IGV xìtǒng de wǔ gè mókuài: shēngchéng, kòngzhì, jìyì, dòngtài hé zhìnéng

5. 最后，文章分析了每个组件的技术挑战和未来方向。 Zuìhòu, wénzhāng fēnxi le měi gè zǔjiàn de jìshù tiǎozhàn hé wèilái fāngxiàng

1. This article introduces Interactive Generative Video (IGV) technology.

2. IGV combines generative and interactive functions to produce high-quality video content, allowing users to participate through control signals and response feedback.

3. The article surveys the applications of IGV in gaming, artificial intelligence, and autonomous driving.

4. It also proposes five modules for an ideal IGV system: generation, control, memory, dynamics, and intelligence.

5. Finally, the article analyzes the technical challenges and future directions for each component.
