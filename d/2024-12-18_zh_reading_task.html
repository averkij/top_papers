Are Your LLMs Capable of Stable Reasoning?

1. 大型语言模型（LLMs）在复杂推理任务中取得了显著进展。

2. 然而，基准测试性能与实际应用之间仍存在差距。

3. 主要原因是当前的评估方法和指标无法充分捕捉LLMs的全部能力，特别是在需要准确性和一致性的复杂推理任务中。

4. 本文提出了G-Pass@k，一个新的评估指标，能够在多次采样中连续评估模型性能，量化模型的最高性能潜力和稳定性。

5. 同时，我们推出了LiveMathBench，一个包含具有挑战性、当代数学问题的动态基准，旨在减少评估过程中的数据泄露风险。

6. 详细结果和基准可访问：https://github.com/open-compass/GPassK。

1. Large language models (LLMs) have made significant strides in complex reasoning tasks.

2. However, there remains a gap between benchmark test performance and real-world applications.

3. The primary reason is that current evaluation methods and metrics fail to fully capture the capabilities of LLMs, especially in complex reasoning tasks that require accuracy and consistency.

4. This paper introduces G-Pass@k, a new evaluation metric that can continuously assess model performance across multiple samples, quantifying the model's peak performance potential and stability.

5. Additionally, we present LiveMathBench, a dynamic benchmark containing challenging, contemporary mathematical problems aimed at reducing the risk of data leakage during the evaluation process.

6. Detailed results and benchmarks are available at: https://github.
