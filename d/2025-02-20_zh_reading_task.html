SuperGPQA: Scaling LLM Evaluation across 285 Graduate Disciplines

1. 大型语言模型（LLMs）在数学、物理和计算机科学等主流学科中表现出显著能力。

2. 然而，人类知识涵盖超过200个专业学科，远超现有基准范围。

3. LLMs在许多这些专业领域，特别是轻工业、农业和服务导向学科的能力尚未充分评估。

4. 为填补这一空白，我们提出SuperGPQA，一个评估285个学科研究生级知识和推理能力的全面基准。

5. 我们的基准采用一种新的人类-LLM协作过滤机制，通过迭代优化LLM响应和专家反馈来消除琐碎或模棱两可的问题。

6. 实验结果显示，当前最先进的LLMs在不同知识领域的表现仍有显著改进空间。

1. Dàxíng yǔyán móxíng (LLMs) zài shùxué, wùlǐ hé jìsuànjī kēxué děng zhǔliú xuékē zhōng biǎoxiàn chū xiǎnzhù nénglì

2. Rán'ér, rénlèi zhīshi hànguǎ chāoguò 200 gè zhuānyè xuékē, yuǎn chāo xiànyǒu jīzhǔn fànwéi

3. LLMs zài xǔduō zhèxiē zhuānyè lǐngyù, tèbié shì qīnggōngyè, nóngyè hé fúwù dǎoxiàng xuékē de nénglì shàng wèi chōngfēn pínggū

4. Wèi tiánbǔ zhè yī kòngbái, wǒmen tíchū SuperGPQA, yīgè pínggǔ 285 gè xuékē yánjiūshēng jí tuí lǐ nénglì de quánmiàn jīzhǔn

6. Shíyàn jiéguǒ xiǎnshì, dāngqián zuì xiānjìn de LLMs zài bùtóng zhīshi lǐngyù de biǎoxiàn réng yǒu xiǎnzhù gǎijìn kōngjiān

1. Large Language Models (LLMs) have demonstrated significant capabilities in mainstream disciplines such as mathematics, physics, and computer science.

2. However, human knowledge encompasses over 200 specialized fields, far exceeding the scope of existing benchmarks.

3. The capabilities of LLMs in many of these specialized areas, particularly in light industries, agriculture, and service-oriented disciplines, have not been adequately evaluated.

4. To address this gap, we propose SuperGPQA, a comprehensive benchmark that assesses graduate-level knowledge and reasoning abilities across 285 disciplines.

5. Our benchmark employs a novel human-LLM collaborative filtering mechanism, which iteratively optimizes LLM responses and expert feedback to eliminate trivial or ambiguous questions.

6. Experimental results indicate that there is still significant room for improvement in the performance of current state-of-the-art LLMs across different knowledge domains.
