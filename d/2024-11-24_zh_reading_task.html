1. 这篇文章讨论了大型扩散模型生成高质量图像，但难以学习新的个性化艺术风格。

2. 现有方法在微调时盲目使用预训练目标和噪声水平分布，导致风格对齐不佳。

3. 作者提出了风格友好的信噪比采样器，在微调时将信噪比分布偏向更高的噪声水平，以捕捉独特风格。

4. 这使模型能更好地生成具有高风格一致性的图像，并允许创建和共享新的风格模板。

1. This article discusses the ability of large diffusion models to generate high-quality images but their difficulty in learning new personalized artistic styles.

2. Existing methods blindly use pre-trained objectives and noise level distributions during fine-tuning, leading to poor style alignment.

3. The authors propose a style-friendly signal-to-noise ratio sampler that biases the signal-to-noise ratio distribution towards higher noise levels during fine-tuning to capture unique styles.

4. This allows the model to generate images with higher style consistency and enables the creation and sharing of new style templates.

5. The authors demonstrate the capability to generate various styles such as watercolor, sketch, and 3D rendering.
