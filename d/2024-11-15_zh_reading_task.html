LLaMA-Mesh: Unifying 3D Mesh Generation with Language Models

1. 这篇文章探讨了扩展大型语言模型（LLMs）生成3D网格的能力。

2. 它利用LLMs中从文本来源（如3D教程）获得的空间知识，并实现对话式3D生成和网格理解。

3. 文章介绍了LLaMA-Mesh，一种将3D网格数据表示为纯文本的新方法，使LLMs能直接处理。

4. 通过监督微调数据集，LLMs可以生成3D网格、产生交替的文本和3D网格输出，并理解和解释3D网格。

5. 这项工作首次展示了LLMs可以通过微调获得复杂的空间知识，生成高质量的3D网格。

1. Zhè piān wénzhāng tàntǎo le kuòzhǎn dàxíng yǔyán móxíng (LLMs) shēngchéng 3D wǎnggé de nénglì

2. Tā lìyòng LLMs zhōng cóng wénběn láiyuán (rú 3D jiàocheng) huòdé kōngjiàn zhīshi, bìng shíxiàn duìhuà shì 3D shēngchéng hé wǎnggé lǐjiě

4. Tōngguò jiāndū wēitiáo shùjùjí, LLMs kěyǐ shēngchéng 3D wǎnggé, chǎnshēng jiāotì de wénběn hé 3D wǎnggé shūrù, bìng lǐjiě hé jiěshì 3D wǎnggé

5. Zhè xiàng gōngzuò shǒucì zhǎnshì LLMs kěyǐ tōngguò wēitiáo huòdé fùzá de kōngjiàn zhīshi, shēngchéng gāo zhìliàng de 3D wǎnggé

1. This article explores the capability of extending large language models (LLMs) to generate 3D meshes.

2. It leverages spatial knowledge derived from textual sources, such as 3D tutorials, within LLMs to achieve conversational 3D generation and mesh understanding.

3. The article introduces LLaMA-Mesh, a new method for representing 3D mesh data as pure text, enabling LLMs to process it directly.

4. Through a supervised fine-tuning dataset, LLMs can generate 3D meshes, produce alternating text and 3D mesh outputs, and understand and interpret 3D meshes.

5. This work demonstrates for the first time that LLMs can acquire complex spatial knowledge through fine-tuning to generate high-quality 3D meshes.
