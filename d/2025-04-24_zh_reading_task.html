2. 然而，当前对多模态大语言模型（MLLMs）的推理评估往往依赖文本描述，允许基于语言的推理捷径，无法衡量真正的视觉中心推理。

3. 为解决这一问题，我们引入了VisuLogic：一个包含1,000个人类验证问题的基准，涵盖六个类别（如量化转换、空间关系、属性比较）。

7. 此外，我们提供了一个补充训练数据集和一个强化学习基线，以支持进一步的进展。

1. Visual reasoning is a core component of human intelligence and a key capability of advanced multimodal models.

2. However, current evaluations of reasoning in multimodal large language models (MLLMs) often rely on textual descriptions, allowing for language-based reasoning shortcuts that fail to measure true vision-centric reasoning.

3. To address this issue, we introduce VisuLogic: a benchmark containing 1,000 human-verified questions covering six categories (such as quantitative transformations, spatial relationships, and attribute comparisons).

4. These different types of questions can evaluate the visual reasoning capabilities of MLLMs from multiple angles.

5. We evaluated leading MLLMs on this benchmark and analyzed the results to identify common failure modes.

6. Most models achieved an accuracy of less than 30%, only slightly above the 25% random baseline and significantly lower than the human accuracy of 51.

7. 4%, indicating a notable gap in visual reasoning.

8. Additionally, we provide a supplementary training dataset and a reinforcement learning baseline to support further advancements.
