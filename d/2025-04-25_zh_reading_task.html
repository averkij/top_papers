1. In recent years, image editing models have made significant progress.

2. The latest multimodal models such as GPT-4o and Gemini2 Flash have demonstrated powerful image editing capabilities.

3. These models can meet the needs of most users, but there is still a gap between open-source algorithms and these closed-source models.

4. Therefore, we have released an image editing model called Step1X-Edit, aiming to provide performance comparable to closed-source models.

5. We use multimodal LLM to process reference images and user instructions, combined with a diffusion image decoder to generate target images.

6. Experimental results show that Step1X-Edit has made important contributions in the field of image editing.
