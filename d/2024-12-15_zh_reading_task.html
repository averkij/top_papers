InternLM-XComposer2.5-OmniLive: A Comprehensive Multimodal System for Long-term Streaming Video and Audio Interactions

1. 这篇文章讨论了创建能够长时间与环境互动的人工智能系统的研究目标。

2. 近期，多模态大型语言模型（MLLMs）在开放世界理解方面取得了显著进展。

3. 然而，连续和同时进行的感知、记忆和推理仍然是一个未探索的挑战。

4. 当前的MLLMs受限于其序列到序列的架构，无法同时处理输入和生成响应。

5. 因此，这个项目引入了分离的感知、推理和记忆机制，使得模型能够实时处理视频和音频输入。

6. 提出的框架IXC2.5-OL包括三个关键模块：实时处理多模态信息的感知模块，整合短期和长期记忆的多模态长期记忆模块，以及响应查询和执行推理任务的推理模块。

7. 这个项目模拟了人类认知，使多模态大型语言模型能够提供持续和适应性的服务。

1. This article discusses the research goal of creating artificial intelligence systems capable of long-term interaction with their environment.

2. Recently, multimodal large language models (MLLMs) have made significant progress in understanding the open world.

3. However, continuous and simultaneous perception, memory, and reasoning remain unexplored challenges.

4. Current MLLMs are limited by their sequence-to-sequence architecture, which prevents them from processing inputs and generating responses simultaneously.

5. Therefore, this project introduces separate mechanisms for perception, reasoning, and memory, allowing the model to process video and audio inputs in real-time.

6. The proposed framework, IXC2.

7. 5-OL, includes three key modules: a perception module for real-time processing of multimodal information, a multimodal long-term memory module that integrates short-term and long-term memory, and a reasoning module for responding to queries and performing reasoning tasks.

8. This project simulates human cognition, enabling multimodal large language models to provide continuous and adaptive services.
