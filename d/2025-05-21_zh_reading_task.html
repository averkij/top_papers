Emerging Properties in Unified Multimodal Pretraining

1. This article introduces an open-source foundational model called BAGEL.

2. It is capable of uniformly processing and generating data in various modalities, such as text, images, and videos.

3. BAGEL, through pre-training on large-scale interleaved multimodal data, demonstrates complex multimodal reasoning capabilities.

4. It outperforms other open-source models in multimodal generation and understanding and possesses advanced multimodal reasoning abilities.

5. The research team has made key findings, pre-training details, data creation protocols, and code and checkpoints publicly available to promote multimodal research.
