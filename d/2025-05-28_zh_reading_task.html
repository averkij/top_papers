The Entropy Mechanism of Reinforcement Learning for Reasoning Language Models

1. 这篇文章旨在解决使用强化学习（RL）和大语言模型（LLMs）进行推理时的一个主要障碍，即策略熵的崩溃。

2. 研究发现，在没有熵干预的情况下，策略熵在训练早期阶段会急剧下降，导致探索能力减弱，策略性能停滞。

3. 文章提出了一个关于熵和下游性能之间的转换方程，并通过理论和实证研究分析了熵动态。

4. 最终，文章提出了两种简单有效的技术来控制熵，实验表明这些方法能够促进探索，帮助策略避免熵崩溃并实现更好的下游性能。

1. This article aims to address a major obstacle in using Reinforcement Learning (RL) and Large Language Models (LLMs) for reasoning: the collapse of policy entropy.

2. Research has found that without entropy intervention, policy entropy drops sharply in the early stages of training, leading to reduced exploration capabilities and stagnation in policy performance.

3. The article proposes a transformation equation between entropy and downstream performance and analyzes entropy dynamics through theoretical and empirical research.

4. Ultimately, the article introduces two simple and effective techniques to control entropy.

5. Experiments show that these methods promote exploration, help policies avoid entropy collapse, and achieve better downstream performance.
