1. This article introduces a new graphical user interface (GUI) assistant model called ShowUI.

2. It combines vision, language, and action to enhance the productivity of human workflows.

3. ShowUI reduces computational costs and improves training efficiency through UI-guided visual mark selection, interleaved vision-language-action flows, and a small-scale, high-quality GUI instruction-following dataset.

4. The model achieved a 75.

5. 1% accuracy rate in zero-shot screenshot localization tasks and performed well in multiple environments.

6. The model's code has been made publicly available on GitHub.
