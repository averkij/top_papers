1. This article introduces a new framework called SNOOPI, aimed at improving the guidance mechanism of single-step diffusion models.

2. Existing methods perform unstably when handling different diffusion model backbones and do not support negative prompt guidance.

3. SNOOPI addresses these issues through the PG-SB and NASA methods.

4. Experimental results demonstrate that SNOOPI significantly enhances the performance of benchmark models, achieving new best-in-class levels.
