VisionZip: Longer is Better but Not Necessary in Vision Language Models

1. 最新的视觉-语言模型通过增加视觉标记的长度提高了性能，但也增加了计算成本。

2. 我们发现，流行的视觉编码器生成的视觉标记中存在大量冗余。

3. 为解决这个问题，我们引入了VisionZip，一种简单有效的方法，选择一组信息丰富的标记输入语言模型，减少冗余并提高效率。

4. VisionZip适用于图像和视频理解任务，特别适合多轮对话场景。

5. 实验结果显示，VisionZip在几乎所有设置中都比以前的最佳方法提高了至少5%的性能。

1. The latest vision-language models have improved performance by increasing the length of visual tokens but have also increased computational costs.

2. We found that popular visual encoders generate visual tokens with a significant amount of redundancy.

3. To address this issue, we introduce VisionZip, a simple and effective method that selects a set of information-rich tokens to input into the language model, reducing redundancy and improving efficiency.

4. VisionZip is applicable to image and video understanding tasks and is particularly suitable for multi-turn conversation scenarios.

5. Experimental results show that VisionZip improves performance by at least 5% over previous best methods in almost all settings.
