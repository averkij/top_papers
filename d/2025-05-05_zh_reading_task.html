Voila: Voice-Language Foundation Models for Real-Time Autonomous Interaction and Voice Role-Play

2. 它能自动、实时且富有情感地与人互动。

3. Voila采用端到端的架构，实现低延迟的全双工对话。

4. 它结合了大语言模型的推理能力和强大的声学建模，支持自然的语音生成。

1. This article introduces a voice AI agent called Voila.

2. It can interact with people automatically, in real-time, and with emotional richness.

3. Voila employs an end-to-end architecture to achieve low-latency, full-duplex conversations.

4. It combines the reasoning capabilities of large language models with powerful acoustic modeling to support natural voice generation.

5. Voila also supports over a million preset voices and efficient customization of new voices.
