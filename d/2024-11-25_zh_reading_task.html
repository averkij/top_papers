Material Anything: Generating Materials for Any 3D Object via Diffusion

1. 我们展示了Material Anything，一个完全自动化、统一的扩散框架，旨在为3D物体生成基于物理的材质。

2. 与依赖复杂流水线或特定案例优化的现有方法不同，Material Anything提供了一个适应多种光照条件下物体的健壮、端到端的解决方案。

3. 我们的方法利用了预训练的图像扩散模型，结合三头架构和渲染损失，以提高稳定性和材质质量。

4. 此外，我们引入了置信掩码作为扩散模型内的动态开关，使其能够有效处理有纹理和无纹理的物体。

5. 通过使用这些置信掩码指导的渐进材质生成策略，以及UV空间材质精炼器，我们的方法确保了一致的、UV准备好的材质输出。

6. 广泛的实验表明，我们的方法在各种物体类别和光照条件下优于现有方法。

1. We presented Material Anything, a fully automated, unified diffusion framework aimed at generating physically-based materials for 3D objects.

2. Unlike existing methods that rely on complex pipelines or case-specific optimizations, Material Anything offers a robust, end-to-end solution adaptable to various lighting conditions for objects.

3. Our approach leverages pre-trained image diffusion models, combined with a tri-head architecture and rendering loss, to enhance stability and material quality.

4. Additionally, we introduced confidence masks as dynamic switches within the diffusion model, enabling it to effectively handle both textured and non-textured objects.

5. By employing a progressive material generation strategy guided by these confidence masks, along with a UV space material refiner, our method ensures consistent, UV-ready material outputs.

6. Extensive experiments demonstrate that our method outperforms existing approaches across various object categories and lighting conditions.
