Enhancing the Reasoning Ability of Multimodal Large Language Models via Mixed Preference Optimization

1. 这篇文章介绍了一种改进多模态大语言模型（MLLMs）的方法。

2. 现有的MLLMs在训练过程中存在分布偏移，影响其多模态推理能力。

3. 作者提出了一种偏好优化（PO）方法来增强MLLMs的多模态推理能力。

4. 具体来说，他们创建了一个高质量的多模态推理偏好数据集MMPR，并开发了一种混合偏好优化（MPO）方法。

5. 这种方法在多个基准测试中表现出色，特别是在多模态推理任务中。

1. Zhè piān wénzhāng jièshào le yīzhǒng gǎijìn duō móshì dà yǔyán móxíng (MLLMs) de fāngfǎ

2. Xiànyǒu de MLLMs zài xùnliàn guòchéng zhōng cúnzài fēnbù piānyí, yǐngxiǎng qí duō móshì tuīlǐ nénglì

3. Zuòzhě tíchū le yīzhǒng piānhǎo yōuhuà (PO) fāngfǎ lái zēngqiáng MLLMs de duō móshì tuīlǐ nénglì

4. Jùtǐ lái shuō, tāmen chuàngjiàn le yīgè gāo zhìliàng de duō móshì tuīlǐ piānhǎo shùjùjí MMPR, bìng kāifā le yīzhǒng hùnjì piānhǎo yōuhuà (MPO) fāngfǎ

1. This article introduces a method for improving multimodal large language models (MLLMs).

2. Existing MLLMs experience distribution shifts during training, which affects their multimodal reasoning capabilities.

3. The authors propose a preference optimization (PO) method to enhance the multimodal reasoning abilities of MLLMs.

4. Specifically, they created a high-quality multimodal reasoning preference dataset called MMPR and developed a mixed preference optimization (MPO) method.

5. This method performs exceptionally well on multiple benchmark tests, particularly in multimodal reasoning tasks.
