DICEPTION: A Generalist Diffusion Model for Visual Perceptual Tasks

1. 这篇文章的主要目标是创建一个计算资源和训练数据有限的多任务通用感知模型。

2. 作者使用了预训练的文本到图像扩散模型。

3. 实验结果表明，DICEPTION 在多个感知任务上表现优异，仅使用了 SAM-vit-h 模型 0.06% 的数据。

4. DICEPTION 通过颜色编码统一了各种感知任务，使得预训练的文本到图像模型可以被充分利用。

5. 在适应其他任务时，模型只需在少量图像和参数上进行微调。

1. The primary objective of this article is to create a general-purpose multi-task perception model with limited computational resources and training data.

2. The authors utilized a pre-trained text-to-image diffusion model.

3. Experimental results demonstrate that DICEPTION performs exceptionally well on multiple perception tasks, using only 0.

4. 06% of the data from the SAM-vit-h model.

5. DICEPTION unifies various perception tasks through color coding, enabling the full utilization of the pre-trained text-to-image model.

6. When adapting to other tasks, the model requires only fine-tuning on a small number of images and parameters.
