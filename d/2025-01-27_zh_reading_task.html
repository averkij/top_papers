Humanity's Last Exam

1. 这篇文章讨论了大型语言模型（LLM）能力评估的重要性。

2. 现有的基准测试已经无法跟上LLM的进步，因为LLM在流行的基准测试上已经达到了90%以上的准确率。

3. 为了解决这个问题，作者引入了“人类最后的考试”（HLE），这是一个跨学科的多模态基准测试。

4. HLE包含3000个问题，涵盖数学、人文学科和自然科学等多个领域。

5. HLE由全球的专家开发，问题具有明确的答案，但无法通过互联网快速找到。

1. This article discusses the importance of evaluating the capabilities of Large Language Models (LLMs).

2. Existing benchmark tests have failed to keep up with the advancements in LLMs, as LLMs have already achieved over 90% accuracy on popular benchmark tests.

3. To address this issue, the authors introduce the "Human's Last Exam" (HLE), a cross-disciplinary, multimodal benchmark test.

4. The HLE consists of 3,000 questions covering various fields such as mathematics, humanities, and natural sciences.

5. Developed by global experts, the questions in the HLE have clear answers but cannot be quickly found on the internet.
