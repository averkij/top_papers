1. This article introduces a 3D perceptual vision-language model called RoboRefer.

2. It can accurately understand complex 3D scenes and perform dynamic instruction reasoning.

3. The model achieves this through supervised fine-tuning and reinforcement fine-tuning.

4. The article also introduces a large-scale dataset, RefSpatial, and an evaluation benchmark, RefSpatial-Bench.

5. Experiments show that RoboRefer excels in spatial understanding and multi-step reasoning tasks, outperforming other benchmarks.
