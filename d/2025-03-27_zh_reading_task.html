Video-R1: Reinforcing Video Reasoning in MLLMs

1. 这篇文章介绍了一种名为Video-R1的新方法，用于在多模态大语言模型中引出视频推理能力。

2. 受DeepSeek-R1成功的启发，研究人员提出了T-GRPO算法来解决视频推理中的时间建模问题，并结合高质量的图像推理数据进行训练。

3. 实验结果显示，Video-R1在多个视频推理基准测试中取得了显著提升，甚至超越了商业专有模型GPT-4o。

4. 所有代码、模型和数据都已公开。

1. This article introduces a new method called Video-R1 for eliciting video reasoning capabilities in multimodal large language models.

2. Inspired by the success of DeepSeek-R1, researchers proposed the T-GRPO algorithm to address the temporal modeling problem in video reasoning and combined it with high-quality image reasoning data for training.

3. Experimental results show that Video-R1 achieved significant improvements in multiple video reasoning benchmark tests, even surpassing the commercial proprietary model GPT-4o.

4. All codes, models, and data have been made publicly available.
