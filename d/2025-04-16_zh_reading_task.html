ColorBench: Can VLMs See and Understand the Colorful World? A Comprehensive Benchmark for Color Perception, Reasoning, and Robustness

1. This article discusses the role of color in how humans and vision-language models (VLMs) perceive and understand the world. It introduces ColorBench, a new benchmark for testing VLMs' color understanding skills. The study finds that while larger models perform better, current VLMs generally neglect color understanding. It also shows that using Chain of Thought (CoT) reasoning can improve color task accuracy. The authors hope ColorBench will help advance research in this area.。
