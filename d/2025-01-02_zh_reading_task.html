OS-Genesis: Automating GUI Agent Trajectory Construction via Reverse Task Synthesis

2. 它通过让代理首先感知环境并进行步进交互，然后逆向推导出高质量任务，从而解决了传统方法中数据收集困难的问题。

1. This article introduces a new method for synthesizing Graphical User Interface (GUI) data, called OS-Genesis.

2. It addresses the challenges of data collection in traditional methods by having agents first perceive the environment and engage in step-by-step interactions, then retroactively deduce high-quality tasks.

3. OS-Genesis employs a trajectory reward model to ensure the quality of the generated trajectories.

4. Experimental results show that GUI agents trained using OS-Genesis perform better on challenging online benchmarks.

5. Further analysis validates the superiority of OS-Genesis in terms of data quality and diversity.
