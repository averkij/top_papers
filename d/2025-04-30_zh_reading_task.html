UniversalRAG: Retrieval-Augmented Generation over Multiple Corpora with Diverse Modalities and Granularities

2. 它能从不同模态和粒度的知识源中检索和整合信息。

3. 现有的RAG方法主要局限于单一模态的语料库，而UniversalRAG通过模态感知路由机制动态选择最合适的模态特定语料库进行检索。

4. 此外，它还根据查询的复杂性和范围，对每种模态进行多级粒度的组织。

5. 研究在8个多模态基准上验证了UniversalRAG的优越性。

1. This article introduces a new Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) framework called UniversalRAG.

2. It can retrieve and integrate information from knowledge sources of different modalities and granularities.

3. Existing RAG methods are mainly limited to single-modality corpora, while UniversalRAG dynamically selects the most suitable modality-specific corpus for retrieval through a modality-aware routing mechanism.

4. Additionally, it organizes each modality at multiple granularity levels based on the complexity and scope of the query.

5. The superiority of UniversalRAG has been validated on 8 multimodal benchmarks.
