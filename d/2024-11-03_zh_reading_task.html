Unpacking SDXL Turbo: Interpreting Text-to-Image Models with Sparse Autoencoders

1. 这篇文章讨论了稀疏自编码器（SAEs）在大语言模型（LLMs）中的应用。

2. SAEs可以将不易解释的中间表示分解成易解释的特征，从而更好地控制和分析。

3. 然而，类似的分析和方法在文本到图像的模型中尚缺乏研究。

4. 作者研究了在少步文本到图像扩散模型（如SDXL Turbo）中使用SAEs学习可解释特征的可能性。

5. 结果发现，SAEs学到的特征可解释，并对生成过程产生因果影响，揭示了模型内部的专业化。

6. 具体来说，有一个模块主要处理图像组合，一个负责添加细节，另一个负责颜色、光照和风格。

7. 因此，这项工作是理解生成文本到图像模型内部的重要一步，展示了SAEs在视觉领域的潜力。

1. This article discusses the application of Sparse Autoencoders (SAEs) in large language models (LLMs).

2. SAEs can decompose hard-to-interpret intermediate representations into interpretable features, thereby enabling better control and analysis.

3. However, similar analyses and methods in text-to-image models are still lacking in research.

4. The authors investigated the possibility of using SAEs to learn interpretable features in few-step text-to-image diffusion models (such as SDXL Turbo).

5. The results showed that the features learned by SAEs are interpretable and have a causal impact on the generation process, revealing specialization within the model.

6. Specifically, one module mainly handles image composition, another is responsible for adding details, and another deals with color, lighting, and style.

7. Therefore, this work is an important step towards understanding the internal workings of text-to-image generation models and demonstrates the potential of SAEs in the visual domain.
