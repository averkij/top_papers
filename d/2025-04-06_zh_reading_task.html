Multi-SWE-bench: A Multilingual Benchmark for Issue Resolving

1. 这篇文章介绍了一个新的多语言问题解决基准，称为Multi-SWE-bench。

2. 它涵盖了Java、TypeScript、JavaScript、Go、Rust、C和C++等语言。

3. 该基准包含1,632个高质量实例，由68位专家注释。

4. 基于这个基准，作者评估了一系列先进的模型，并提供了详细的分析。

5. 此外，文章还宣布成立了一个开源社区Multi-SWE-RL，旨在为问题解决任务构建大规模强化学习训练数据集。

1. This article introduces a new multilingual problem-solving benchmark called Multi-SWE-bench.

2. It covers languages such as Java, TypeScript, JavaScript, Go, Rust, C, and C++.

3. The benchmark contains 1,632 high-quality instances annotated by 68 experts.

4. Based on this benchmark, the authors evaluated a series of advanced models and provided detailed analyses.

5. Additionally, the article announces the establishment of an open-source community, Multi-SWE-RL, aimed at building large-scale reinforcement learning training datasets for problem-solving tasks.
