ProRL: Prolonged Reinforcement Learning Expands Reasoning Boundaries in Large Language Models

1. 这篇文章讨论了强化学习（RL）在语言模型推理能力中的作用。

2. 研究表明，延长RL训练可以发现基础模型无法访问的新推理策略。

3. 作者提出了ProRL方法，结合KL散度控制和多样化任务。

4. 实验结果显示，RL训练的模型在多种评估中表现优于基础模型。

5. 推理边界的改进与基础模型的任务能力和训练时长密切相关。

1. This article discusses the role of reinforcement learning (RL) in the reasoning capabilities of language models.

2. Research indicates that extending RL training can uncover new reasoning strategies that the base model cannot access.

3. The authors propose the ProRL method, which combines KL divergence control and diverse tasks.

4. Experimental results show that RL-trained models outperform the base model in various evaluations.

5. Improvements in reasoning boundaries are closely related to the task capabilities and training duration of the base model.
