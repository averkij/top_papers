Agent-R: Training Language Model Agents to Reflect via Iterative Self-Training

1. 这篇文章讨论了大语言模型（LLMs）在处理复杂任务中的重要性。

2. 现有方法主要通过模仿更强的专家来提高性能，但这些方法在实际应用中常常失败，因为无法从错误中恢复。

3. 文章提出了一种迭代自训练框架，Agent-R，使语言代理能够在执行过程中进行反思。

4. 通过使用蒙特卡罗树搜索（MCTS），Agent-R 能够从错误路径中恢复正确路径。

5. 实验结果显示，Agent-R 能够有效地纠正错误行为，并在三个互动环境中表现优于基线方法。

1. This article discusses the importance of large language models (LLMs) in handling complex tasks.

2. Existing methods primarily enhance performance by mimicking stronger experts, but these methods often fail in practical applications because they cannot recover from errors.

3. The article proposes an iterative self-training framework, Agent-R, which enables language agents to reflect during the execution process.

4. By utilizing Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS), Agent-R can recover from incorrect paths to the correct ones.

5. Experimental results demonstrate that Agent-R can effectively correct erroneous behaviors and outperforms baseline methods in three interactive environments.
