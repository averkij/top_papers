ReCamMaster: Camera-Controlled Generative Rendering from A Single Video

1. 这篇文章讨论了一种名为ReCamMaster的视频重新渲染框架。

2. 它可以改变给定视频的摄像机轨迹，同时保持多帧外观和动态同步。

3. 该方法利用了预训练的文本到视频模型的生成能力，并通过Unreal Engine 5构建了一个多摄像机同步视频数据集。

4. 实验结果显示，这种方法在视频稳定、超分辨率和视频扩展方面表现出色。

1. This article discusses a video re-rendering framework called ReCamMaster.

2. It can alter the camera trajectory of a given video while maintaining multi-frame appearance and dynamic synchronization.

3. The method leverages the generative capabilities of a pre-trained text-to-video model and constructs a multi-camera synchronized video dataset using Unreal Engine 5.

4. Experimental results demonstrate that this method performs excellently in video stabilization, super-resolution, and video extension.
