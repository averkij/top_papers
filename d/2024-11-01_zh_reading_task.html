A Pointer Network-based Approach for Joint Extraction and Detection of Multi-Label Multi-Class Intents

1. 这篇文章讨论了任务导向对话系统中的意图检测。

2. 现有研究主要处理单一意图的简单查询，缺乏处理多意图复杂查询的系统。

3. 此外，缺少多语言、多意图的数据集。

4. 该研究解决了三个关键任务：从查询中提取多意图跨度、检测多意图、开发多语言多标签意图数据集。

5. 研究介绍了一个新的多标签多类别意图检测数据集和一个基于指针网络的架构，用于提取意图跨度和检测多意图。

6. 分析显示，该系统在准确性和F1分数上优于基准方法。

1. This article discusses intent detection in task-oriented dialogue systems.

2. Existing research primarily addresses simple queries with single intents, lacking systems that handle complex queries with multiple intents.

3. Additionally, there is a shortage of multilingual, multi-intent datasets.

4. The research addresses three key tasks: extracting multiple intent spans from queries, detecting multiple intents, and developing a multilingual, multi-label intent dataset.

5. The study introduces a new multi-label, multi-class intent detection dataset and a pointer network-based architecture for extracting intent spans and detecting multiple intents.

6. Analysis shows that the system outperforms baseline methods in terms of accuracy and F1 score.
