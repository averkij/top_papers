Qwen2.5 Technical Report

1. 这篇文章介绍了Qwen2.5，一系列大型语言模型。

2. 与前一版本相比，Qwen2.5在预训练和后训练阶段都有显著改进。

3. 预训练数据集从7万亿个标记扩展到18万亿个，增强了常识、专业知识和推理能力。

4. 后训练使用了超过100万个样本的监督微调和多阶段强化学习，提升了人类偏好和长文本生成等能力。

5. Qwen2.5系列提供多种尺寸和版本，包括基础模型和指令微调模型，以及量化版本。

1. This article introduces Qwen2.

2. 5, a series of large language models.

3. Compared to the previous version, Qwen2.

4. 5 has significant improvements in both the pre-training and post-training stages.

5. The pre-training dataset has been expanded from 7 trillion tokens to 18 trillion tokens, enhancing common sense, professional knowledge, and reasoning capabilities.

6. Post-training involved supervised fine-tuning with over 1 million samples and multi-stage reinforcement learning, improving human preference and long text generation capabilities.

7. The Qwen2.

8. 5 series offers various sizes and versions, including base models, instruction-tuned models, and quantized versions.
