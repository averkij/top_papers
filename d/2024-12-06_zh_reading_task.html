VisionZip: Longer is Better but Not Necessary in Vision Language Models

1. 最近视觉-语言模型的进展通过增加视觉标记的长度来提高性能，但这也增加了计算成本。

2. 研究发现，流行的视觉编码器生成的视觉标记中存在大量冗余。

3. 为解决这一问题，研究人员提出了VisionZip方法，选择一组有用的视觉标记输入语言模型，减少冗余并提高效率。

4. 实验结果显示，VisionZip在几乎所有设置中都比之前的最佳方法提高了至少5%的性能。

5. 此外，该方法显著提高了模型推理速度，使LLaVA-Next 13B模型比LLaVA-Next 7B模型推理更快，并取得更好的结果。

1. Recent advancements in vision-language models have improved performance by increasing the length of visual tokens, but this has also increased computational costs.

2. Research has found that popular visual encoders generate visual tokens with a significant amount of redundancy.

3. To address this issue, researchers have proposed the VisionZip method, which selects a set of useful visual tokens to input into the language model, reducing redundancy and enhancing efficiency.

4. Experimental results show that VisionZip improves performance by at least 5% over previous best methods in almost all settings.

5. Additionally, this method significantly speeds up model inference, making the LLaVA-Next 13B model infer faster than the LLaVA-Next 7B model and achieve better results.
