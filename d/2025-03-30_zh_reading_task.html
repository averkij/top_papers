Think Before Recommend: Unleashing the Latent Reasoning Power for Sequential Recommendation

1. This article introduces a new recommendation system framework called ReaRec.

2. It aims to enhance user representation through multi-step reasoning, thereby better capturing the complex changes in user preferences.

3. ReaRec employs an autoregressive approach to feed the final hidden state of the sequence back into the recommendation system and introduces two lightweight reasoning learning methods.

4. Experimental results demonstrate that ReaRec can significantly improve the performance of various sequential recommendation models.

5. The authors believe that this work opens up new directions for future research in recommendation systems.
