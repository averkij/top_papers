1. This article discusses the research goals of creating artificial intelligence systems that can interact with the environment for extended periods.

2. Recently, multimodal large language models (MLLMs) have made significant progress in understanding the open world.

3. However, simultaneously performing continuous perception, memory, and reasoning remains an insufficiently explored challenge.

4. Current MLLMs, due to their sequence-to-sequence architecture, are unable to process inputs and generate responses concurrently.

5. Therefore, this project introduces separate mechanisms for perception, reasoning, and memory, enabling the model to process video and audio inputs in real-time.
