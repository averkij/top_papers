One-Step Residual Shifting Diffusion for Image Super-Resolution via Distillation

1. 这篇文章讨论了超分辨率（SR）扩散模型的计算成本问题。

2. 虽然有些方法可以加速SR模型，但它们要么失去细节，要么产生不存在的结构。

3. 为解决这些问题，作者提出了RSD，一种新的蒸馏方法。

4. RSD在单步恢复中超越了教师模型，并在多个数据集上展示了优异的表现。

1. This article discusses the computational cost issues of super-resolution (SR) diffusion models.

2. Although some methods can accelerate SR models, they either lose detail or generate non-existent structures.

3. To address these problems, the authors propose RSD, a new distillation method.

4. RSD surpasses the teacher model in single-step recovery and demonstrates excellent performance across multiple datasets.

5. Compared to other methods, RSD offers better perceptual quality and requires fewer parameters.
