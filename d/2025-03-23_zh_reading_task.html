1. 这篇文章讨论了多模态科学问题（MSPs）及其挑战。 Zhè piān wénzhāng tǎolùn le duō móshuài kēxué wèntí (MSPs) jí qí tiǎozhàn

3. 目前，MSPs面临两大问题：多模态推理和缺乏反思能力。 Mùqián, MSPs miànlín liǎng dà wèntí: duō móshuài tuīlǐ hé quēfá fǎnsī nénglì

4. 作者提出了基于大七人格和苏格拉底指导的多代理框架（MAPS）来解决这些问题。 Zuòzhě tíchū le jīyú dà qī réngé hé Sūgélādǐ zhǐdǎo de duō dàilǐ kuàngjià (MAPS) lái jiějué zhèxiē wèntí

5. 实验结果显示，该框架在多个数据集上表现出色，超越了现有的最佳模型。 Shíyàn jiéguǒ xiǎnshì, gǎi kuàngjià zài duō gè shùjùjí shàng biǎoxiàn chūsè, chāoyuè le xiànyǒu de zuìjiā móxíng

1. This article discusses multimodal scientific problems (MSPs) and their challenges.

2. MSPs require the integration of multiple modalities, such as text and graphs.

3. Currently, MSPs face two major issues: multimodal reasoning and a lack of reflective capabilities.

4. The authors propose a multi-agent framework (MAPS) based on the Big Five personality traits and Socratic guidance to address these issues.

5. Experimental results show that this framework performs excellently on multiple datasets, surpassing existing state-of-the-art models.
