1. VS-Bench is a multimodal benchmark designed to evaluate the strategic reasoning and decision-making capabilities of vision-language models in complex multi-agent environments.

2. Existing benchmarks are largely limited to single-agent or text-only environments, whereas VS-Bench includes eight visually-based environments that encompass cooperative, competitive, and mixed-motive interactions.

3. Research has revealed significant gaps in the predictive accuracy and normalized returns of existing models.

4. VS-Bench aims to standardize evaluations and highlight the limitations of current models, driving future research.

5. The code and data are available at https://vs-bench.
