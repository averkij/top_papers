MIG: Automatic Data Selection for Instruction Tuning by Maximizing Information Gain in Semantic Space

1. 这篇文章讨论了构建有效指令调整数据集的关键因素：数据质量和多样性。

2. 随着开源数据集的增加，自动选择高质量且多样的子集变得重要。

3. 现有方法主要关注实例质量，使用启发式规则维持多样性，但 often 效果不佳。

4. 作者提出了一种新方法，通过构建标签图来模拟语义空间，并基于图中的信息分布量化多样性。

5. 实验显示，这种方法在多个数据集和基础模型上都优于现有方法。

1. This article discusses the key factors in constructing an effective dataset for instruction tuning: data quality and diversity.

2. As the number of open-source datasets increases, it becomes important to automatically select high-quality and diverse subsets.

3. Existing methods primarily focus on instance quality, using heuristic rules to maintain diversity, but often with limited effectiveness.

4. The authors propose a new method that constructs a label graph to simulate semantic space and quantifies diversity based on the distribution of information in the graph.

5. Experiments show that this method outperforms existing methods across multiple datasets and base models.
