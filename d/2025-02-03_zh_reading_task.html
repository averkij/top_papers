The Differences Between Direct Alignment Algorithms are a Blur

1. 这篇文章介绍了直接对齐算法（DAAs），它通过直接策略优化简化了语言模型对齐。

2. DAAs可以根据排名损失、奖励类型或是否需要监督微调阶段来分类。

3. 文章指出，单阶段方法表现不如双阶段方法，但通过添加显式的监督微调阶段和引入beta参数，可以提高其性能。

4. 分析显示，关键因素在于使用的是成对目标还是点目标，而不是具体的隐式奖励或损失函数。

5. 结果强调了仔细评估的重要性，以避免过早声称性能提升或整体优越性。

1. This article introduces Direct Alignment Algorithms (DAAs), which simplify language model alignment through direct strategy optimization.

2. DAAs can be classified based on ranking loss, reward type, or whether a supervised fine-tuning stage is required.

3. The article notes that single-stage methods perform less well than dual-stage methods, but their performance can be improved by adding an explicit supervised fine-tuning stage and introducing a beta parameter.

4. The analysis indicates that the key factor lies in the use of paired targets versus point targets, rather than specific implicit rewards or loss functions.

5. The results emphasize the importance of careful evaluation to avoid premature claims of performance improvement or overall superiority.
