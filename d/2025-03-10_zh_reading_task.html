Feature-Level Insights into Artificial Text Detection with Sparse Autoencoders

2. 尽管有许多努力，但没有一个算法能在不同类型的未知文本中表现一致，或保证有效地泛化到新的LLMs。

3. 可解释性在实现这一目标中起着关键作用。

4. 在这项研究中，我们通过使用稀疏自编码器（SAE）从Gemma-2-2b残差流中提取特征来增强ATD的可解释性。

1. With the advancement of large language models (LLMs), artificial text detection (ATD) has become increasingly important.

2. Despite numerous efforts, no single algorithm has been able to perform consistently across different types of unknown texts or guarantee effective generalization to new LLMs.

3. Explainability plays a crucial role in achieving this goal.

4. In this research, we enhance the explainability of ATD by extracting features from the residual stream of Gemma-2-2b using sparse autoencoders (SAE).

5. We identify interpretable and efficient features and analyze their semantics and relevance through domain- and model-specific statistics, guided methods, and manual or LLM-based explanations.

6. Our approach provides valuable insights into the differences between texts generated by different models and human-written content.

7. We demonstrate that modern LLMs have a unique writing style in information-intensive domains, even though they can generate human-like outputs through personalized prompts.
