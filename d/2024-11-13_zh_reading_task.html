SAMPart3D: Segment Any Part in 3D Objects

1. 这篇文章讨论了3D部件分割的重要性及其在机器人、3D生成和3D编辑中的应用。

2. 现有方法利用视觉语言模型进行2D到3D的知识蒸馏，实现零样本3D部件分割，但依赖文本提示，限制了扩展性和处理部件歧义的灵活性。

3. 文章介绍了SAMPart3D，一个可扩展的零样本3D部件分割框架，不需要预定义的部件标签集。

4. 它使用与文本无关的视觉基础模型进行蒸馏，并提取多尺度的部件感知3D特征。

5. 实验显示，SAMPart3D在大规模3D数据集上表现优异，并超越现有方法。

1. This article discusses the importance of 3D part segmentation and its applications in robotics, 3D generation, and 3D editing.

2. Existing methods utilize vision-language models for knowledge distillation from 2D to 3D, achieving zero-shot 3D part segmentation, but they rely on textual prompts, which limits their scalability and flexibility in handling part ambiguities.

3. The article introduces SAMPart3D, a scalable zero-shot 3D part segmentation framework that does not require predefined part labels.

4. It uses a text-agnostic visual foundation model for distillation and extracts multi-scale part-aware 3D features.

5. Experiments show that SAMPart3D performs excellently on large-scale 3D datasets and outperforms existing methods.
