1. This article introduces a new framework called LUFFY for improving large-scale reasoning models.

2. LUFFY combines off-policy demonstration and on-policy expansion, dynamically balancing imitation and exploration.

3. It avoids superficial and rigid imitation through regularized importance sampling.

4. LUFFY has achieved significant advancements in six mathematical benchmark tests and performs excellently on out-of-distribution tasks.

5. Analysis shows that LUFFY not only effectively imitates but also explores beyond the demonstration.
