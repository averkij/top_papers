DropletVideo: A Dataset and Approach to Explore Integral Spatio-Temporal Consistent Video Generation

1. 这篇文章讨论了视频生成中的时空一致性。

2. 它指出，合格的生成视频必须确保情节合理性和连贯性，同时保持物体和场景在不同视角下的视觉一致性。

3. 过去的研究主要集中在时间或空间一致性上，或者它们的基本结合。

4. 然而，摄像机移动可能引入新物体或消除现有物体，从而影响前面的叙述。

5. 本文介绍并研究了整体时空一致性，考虑了情节进展和摄像技术之间的协同作用，以及先前内容对后续生成的长期影响。

6. 研究包括数据集构建和模型开发。

7. 首先，构建了包含1000万个视频的DropletVideo-10M数据集，每个视频平均有206个字的描述。

8. 然后，开发并训练了DropletVideo模型，擅长在视频生成过程中保持时空连贯性。

9. 数据集和模型可以在https://dropletx.github.io访问。

1. This article discusses the spatiotemporal consistency in video generation.

2. It points out that a qualified generated video must ensure the reasonableness and coherence of the plot while maintaining the visual consistency of objects and scenes from different angles.

3. Previous research has mainly focused on temporal or spatial consistency, or their basic combination.

4. However, camera movements may introduce new objects or remove existing ones, affecting the previous narrative.

5. This paper introduces and studies overall spatiotemporal consistency, considering the synergy between plot development and cinematographic techniques, as well as the long-term impact of previous content on subsequent generation.

6. The research includes the construction of a dataset and model development.

7. First, the DropletVideo-10M dataset was constructed, containing 10 million videos, each with an average description of 206 words.

8. Then, the DropletVideo model was developed and trained, excelling in maintaining spatiotemporal coherence during the video generation process.

9. The dataset and model can be accessed at https://dropletx.
