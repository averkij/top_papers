CORAL: Benchmarking Multi-turn Conversational Retrieval-Augmentation Generation

1. 这篇文章介绍了检索增强生成（RAG），一种通过外部知识检索增强大型语言模型的强大范式。

2. 尽管RAG受到广泛关注，但现有研究主要集中在单轮对话上，忽略了现实应用中复杂的多轮对话。

3. 为填补这一空白，作者提出了CORAL，一个大规模基准数据集，用于评估多轮对话中的RAG系统。

4. CORAL包含多样的信息寻求对话，涵盖开放域、知识密集度、自由形式回复和话题转换等挑战。

5. 它支持对话RAG的三个核心任务：段落检索、回复生成和引用标记。

6. 作者提出了一个统一框架，并在CORAL上对各种对话RAG方法进行了综合评估，展示了改进现有方法的巨大潜力。

1. This article introduces Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), a powerful paradigm that enhances large language models through external knowledge retrieval.

2. Although RAG has received widespread attention, existing research primarily focuses on single-turn conversations, overlooking the complex multi-turn conversations encountered in real-world applications.

3. To address this gap, the authors propose CORAL, a large-scale benchmark dataset for evaluating RAG systems in multi-turn conversations.

4. CORAL includes diverse information-seeking dialogues, encompassing challenges such as open domains, knowledge intensity, free-form responses, and topic shifts.

5. It supports three core tasks for conversational RAG: paragraph retrieval, response generation, and citation marking.

6. The authors present a unified framework and conduct a comprehensive evaluation of various conversational RAG methods on CORAL, demonstrating the significant potential for improving existing methods.
