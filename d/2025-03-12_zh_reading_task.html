1. This article introduces YuE, an open-source foundational model based on the LLaMA2 architecture.

2. It can generate music up to five minutes long, maintaining lyric alignment, coherent musical structure, and captivating vocal melodies.

3. YuE achieves this through a multi-task, multi-stage pre-training method.

4. It is also capable of style transfer and bidirectional generation.

5. Experiments show that YuE matches or surpasses some proprietary systems in terms of musicality and vocal flexibility.

6. Additionally, YuE performs excellently in music understanding tasks.
