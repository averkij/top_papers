3. 然而，这些行为的时间和一致性难以预测和控制。

4. 为了解决这些问题，作者提出了一种新方法，明确地将模型与三种元能力对齐：演绎、归纳和溯因。

5. 这种方法提高了性能，并在数学、编程和科学基准测试中显示出显著的改进。

1. This article discusses the potential capabilities of large reasoning models (LRMs).

2. Previous research has shown that result-based reinforcement learning (RL) can occasionally trigger advanced reasoning behaviors, such as self-correction and verification phenomena, referred to as the model's "eureka moments.

3. " However, the timing and consistency of these behaviors are difficult to predict and control.

4. To address these issues, the authors propose a new method that explicitly aligns the model with three meta-abilities: deduction, induction, and abduction.

5. This method improves performance and demonstrates significant improvements in mathematics, programming, and scientific benchmark tests.
