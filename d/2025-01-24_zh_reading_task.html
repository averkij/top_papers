1. 我们介绍了Sigma，一种针对系统领域的高效大语言模型。它采用了包括DiffQKV注意力机制在内的新架构，并在我们精心收集的系统领域数据上进行了预训练。DiffQKV注意力机制通过对Query、Key和Value组件进行差分优化，显著提高了推理效率。Sigma在系统领域的表现优于GPT-4，在通用领域也与其他最先进的模型表现相当。 Wǒmen jièshào le Sigma, yī zhǒng zhēnduì xìtǒng lǐngyù de gāoxiào dà yǔyán móxíng

2. Tā cǎiyòng le bāokuò DiffQKV zhùyìlì jīzhì zài nèi de xīn jiàgòu, bìng zài wǒmen jīngxīn shōují de xìtǒng lǐngyù shùjù shàng jìnxíng le yùxùnliàn

3. DiffQKV zhùyìlì jīzhì tōngguò duì Query, Key hé Value zǔjiàn jìnxíng chāfēn yōuhuà, xiǎnzhù tīgāo le tuīlǐ xiàolǜ

4. Sigma zài xìtǒng lǐngyù de biǎoxiàn yōuyú GPT-4, zài tōngyòng lǐngyù yě yǔ qítā zuì xiānjìn de móxíng biǎoxiàn xiāngdāng

1. We introduced Sigma, a highly efficient large language model tailored for the systems domain.

2. It incorporates a new architecture that includes the DiffQKV attention mechanism and has been pre-trained on our carefully curated systems domain data.

3. The DiffQKV attention mechanism significantly enhances inference efficiency by applying differential optimization to the Query, Key, and Value components.

4. Sigma outperforms GPT-4 in the systems domain and performs comparably to other state-of-the-art models in the general domain.
