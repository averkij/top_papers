StdGEN: Semantic-Decomposed 3D Character Generation from Single Images

1. 这篇文章介绍了StdGEN，一种创新的从单张图像生成高质量3D角色的方法。

2. 它能在三分钟内生成具有分离的语义组件（如身体、衣服和头发）的详细3D角色。

3. StdGEN的核心是提出的语义感知大型重建模型（S-LRM），能够从多视图图像中重建几何、颜色和语义。

4. 实验证明，StdGEN在3D动漫角色生成方面表现出色，优于现有方法。

5. 它为虚拟现实、游戏和电影制作等提供了灵活的定制化3D角色。

1. This article introduces StdGEN, an innovative method for generating high-quality 3D characters from a single image.

2. It can produce detailed 3D characters with separate semantic components (such as body, clothing, and hair) in just three minutes.

3. The core of StdGEN is the proposed semantic-aware large reconstruction model (S-LRM), which can reconstruct geometry, color, and semantics from multi-view images.

4. Experiments have shown that StdGEN performs excellently in generating 3D animated characters, outperforming existing methods.

5. It provides flexible customization of 3D characters for virtual reality, gaming, and film production.
