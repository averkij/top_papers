DDT: Decoupled Diffusion Transformer

1. 扩散变压器在生成质量上表现出色，但需要更长的训练迭代和多次推理步骤。

2. 每个去噪步骤中，扩散变压器编码噪声输入以提取低频语义成分，然后解码高频成分。

3. 这种方案导致优化困境：编码低频语义需要减少高频成分，造成语义编码和高频解码之间的矛盾。

4. 我们提出一种新的解耦扩散变压器（DDT），具有专门的条件编码器和速度解码器。

5. 实验显示，更大的编码器随着模型规模增加提高性能。

6. 在ImageNet 256x256上，DDT-XL/2达到1.31 FID（训练收敛速度近乎快4倍）；在ImageNet 512x512上，达到1.28 FID。

7. 此外，解耦架构提高推理速度，允许相邻去噪步骤间共享自我条件。

8. 我们提出一种新的统计动态规划方法来最小化性能下降。

1. The diffusion transformer performs excellently in terms of generation quality but requires longer training iterations and multiple inference steps.

2. In each denoising step, the diffusion transformer encodes noisy input to extract low-frequency semantic components and then decodes high-frequency components.

3. This scheme leads to an optimization dilemma: encoding low-frequency semantics requires reducing high-frequency components, creating a conflict between semantic encoding and high-frequency decoding.

4. We propose a new decoupled diffusion transformer (DDT) with dedicated conditional encoders and velocity decoders.

5. Experiments show that larger encoders improve performance as the model scale increases.

6. On ImageNet 256x256, DDT-XL/2 achieves 1.

7. 31 FID (with training convergence speed nearly 4 times faster); on ImageNet 512x512, it achieves 1.

8. 28 FID.

9. Additionally, the decoupled architecture increases inference speed, allowing adjacent denoising steps to share self-conditioning.

10. We propose a new statistical dynamic programming method to minimize performance degradation.
