Competitive Programming with Large Reasoning Models

1. 我们展示了强化学习应用于大型语言模型（LLMs）可以显著提升复杂编程和推理任务的表现。

2. 我们比较了两个通用推理模型 - OpenAI o1 和 o3 的早期版本，以及一个针对2024年国际信息学奥林匹克（IOI）设计的特定领域系统 o1-ioi。

3. 我们在IOI 2024上使用 o1-ioi 参赛，并在放宽的比赛约束下获得了金牌。

4. 然而，后续模型如 o3 在没有手工制定的特定领域策略或放宽约束的情况下也能获得金牌。

5. 我们的发现表明，虽然专门的流水线如 o1-ioi 带来了显著的改进，但扩展的通用 o3 模型在不依赖手工制定的推理启发式的情况下超越了这些结果。

1. Wǒmen zhǎnshìle qiáng huà xuéxí yìngyòng yú dàxíng yǔyán móxíng (LLMs) kěyǐ xiǎnzhù tíshēng fùzá bǐan chéng yǔ tuīlǐ rènwù de biǎoxiàn

2. Wǒmen bǐjiàole liǎng gè tōngyòng tuīlǐ móxíng - OpenAI o1 hé o3 de zǎoqī bǎnběn, yǐjià yīgè zhǐduì 2024 nián guójì xìnxī xué àolínpǐkè (IOI) shèjì de tèdìng yùyí xìtǒng o1-ioi

3. Wǒmen zài IOI 2024 shàng shǐyòng o1-ioi cānsài, bìng zài fàngkuān de bǐsài yuēshù xià huòdéle jīnpái

5. Wǒmen de fāxiàn biǎomíng, suīrán zhuānmén de liúshuǐxiàn rú o1-ioi dàilái le xiǎnzhù de gǎijìn, dàn kuòzhǎn de tōngyòng o3 móxíng zài bù yīlài shǒugōng zhìdìng de tuīlǐ qǐfǎshì de qíngkuàng xià chāoyuèle zhèxiē jiéguǒ

1. We demonstrated that applying reinforcement learning to large language models (LLMs) can significantly enhance performance in complex programming and reasoning tasks.

2. We compared two general reasoning models—early versions of OpenAI o1 and o3—and a domain-specific system, o1-ioi, designed for the 2024 International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI).

3. We competed in IOI 2024 using o1-ioi and won a gold medal under relaxed competition constraints.

4. However, subsequent models like o3 were able to achieve gold medals without handcrafted domain-specific strategies or relaxed constraints.

5. Our findings indicate that while specialized pipelines like o1-ioi brought significant improvements, the expanded general o3 model surpassed these results without relying on handcrafted reasoning heuristics.
