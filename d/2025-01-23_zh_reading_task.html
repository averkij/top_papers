1. We introduced two reasoning models: DeepSeek-R1-Zero and DeepSeek-R1.

2. DeepSeek-R1-Zero, trained through large-scale reinforcement learning, demonstrated excellent reasoning capabilities but suffered from poor readability and language mixing issues.

3. To address these problems, we developed DeepSeek-R1, which undergoes multi-stage training and cold start data processing before reinforcement learning.

4. DeepSeek-R1 performs comparably to OpenAI-o1-1217 in reasoning tasks.

5. We have open-sourced these models along with six compressed models based on Qwen and Llama.
