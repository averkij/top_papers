DeepMesh: Auto-Regressive Artist-mesh Creation with Reinforcement Learning

1. 三角网格在3D应用中扮演重要角色，用于高效操作和渲染。

2. 自回归方法通过预测离散顶点标记生成结构化网格，但往往受到有限面数和网格不完整的限制。

3. 我们提出了DeepMesh框架，通过两项关键创新优化网格生成：(1) 高效的预训练策略，包含新的标记算法，以及数据整理和处理的改进；(2) 引入强化学习（RL）到3D网格生成，通过直接偏好优化（DPO）实现人类偏好对齐。

4. 我们设计了一个结合人类评估和3D度量的评分标准，收集DPO的偏好对，确保视觉吸引力和几何精度。

5. 基于点云和图像，DeepMesh生成具有复杂细节和精确拓扑的网格，在精度和质量上超越现有方法。

6. 项目页面：https://zhaorw02.github.io/DeepMesh/。

1. Triangular meshes play a crucial role in 3D applications, enabling efficient operations and rendering.

2. Autoregressive methods generate structured meshes by predicting discrete vertex labels but are often constrained by limited face counts and incomplete meshes.

3. We introduce the DeepMesh framework, which optimizes mesh generation through two key innovations: (1) an efficient pre-training strategy that includes a new labeling algorithm, as well as improvements in data curation and processing; (2) the introduction of reinforcement learning (RL) into 3D mesh generation, achieving human preference alignment through direct preference optimization (DPO).

4. We designed a scoring standard that combines human evaluation and 3D metrics to collect DPO preference pairs, ensuring visual appeal and geometric accuracy.

5. Based on point clouds and images, DeepMesh generates meshes with complex details and precise topology, surpassing existing methods in both accuracy and quality.
