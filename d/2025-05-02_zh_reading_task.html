1. This article introduces Interactive Generative Video (IGV) technology.

2. IGV combines generative and interactive capabilities to produce high-quality video content, with applications in gaming, artificial intelligence, and autonomous driving.

3. The article analyzes the five key modules of IGV and the technical challenges it faces.

4. The aim is to advance future research and development of IGV technology.
