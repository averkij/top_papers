1. This article introduces a new large language model called START, which combines external tools to enhance its capabilities in complex reasoning tasks.

2. Traditional models such as OpenAI-o1 and DeepSeek-R1 often suffer from hallucinations and inefficiencies due to their reliance on internal reasoning.

3. START addresses these issues by executing code, performing complex calculations, self-checking, exploring multiple methods, and self-debugging.

4. Its core innovation lies in a self-learning framework that includes two technologies: Hint-infer and Hint-RFT.

5. START demonstrates excellent performance in high-difficulty scientific question-answering and mathematical competitions, significantly outperforming the baseline model QwQ-32B.
