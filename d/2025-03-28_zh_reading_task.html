Video-R1: Reinforcing Video Reasoning in MLLMs

2. 直接应用RL训练和GRPO算法存在两个主要挑战：缺乏视频推理的时间建模和高质量视频推理数据的稀缺。

1. This article introduces Video-R1, the first systematic attempt to explore the R1 paradigm to stimulate video reasoning capabilities in multimodal large language models.

2. Directly applying RL training and the GRPO algorithm presents two major challenges: the lack of temporal modeling for video reasoning and the scarcity of high-quality video reasoning data.

3. To address these issues, the authors propose the T-GRPO algorithm and combine it with high-quality image reasoning data for training.

4. Experimental results show that Video-R1 achieves significant improvements on multiple video reasoning benchmarks.
