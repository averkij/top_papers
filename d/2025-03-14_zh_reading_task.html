1. This article discusses the role of normalization layers in modern neural networks.

2. Research has found that even without using normalization layers, Transformers can achieve the same or better performance through a simple technique called Dynamic Tanh (DyT), which is an element-wise operation.

3. DyT is inspired by the observation that layer normalization in Transformers often produces tanh-like S-shaped input-output mappings.

4. By using DyT, Transformers can match or surpass the performance of those with normalization layers without needing them.
