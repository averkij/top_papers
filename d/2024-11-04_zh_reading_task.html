OS-ATLAS: A Foundation Action Model for Generalist GUI Agents

1. 这篇文章讨论了构建图形用户界面（GUI）代理的现有努力。

2. 目前主要依赖于强大的商业视觉语言模型（VLMs），如GPT-4o和GeminiProVision。

3. 开源VLMs在GUI理解和未见分布（OOD）场景中表现较差，因此实践者不太愿意使用。

4. 为了推动这一领域的研究，作者开发了OS-Atlas，这是一个擅长GUI理解和OOD任务的基础模型。

5. 他们还发布了一个包含1300多万GUI元素的跨平台数据集，并证明了OS-Atlas在多个基准测试中的显著性能提升。

1. Zhè piān wénzhāng tǎolùn le gòujiàn túxíng yònghù jiēmiàn (GUI) dàilǐ de xiàn yǒu nǔlì

2. Dāngqián zhǔyào yīlài zài qiángdà de shāngyè shìjué yǔyán móxíng (VLMs) shàng, rú GPT-4o hé GeminiProVision

3. Kāiyuán VLMs zài GUI lǐjiě hé wèi jiàn fēnbù (OOD) chǎngjǐng zhōng biǎoxiǎn jiàochǎ, yīncǐ shíjiànzhě bù tài yuànyì shǐyòng

4. Wèile tuīdòng zhè yī lǐngyù de yánjiū, zuòzhě kāifāle OS-Atlas, zhè shì yīgè shàncháng GUI lǐjiě hé OOD rènwù de jīchǔ móxíng

5. Tāmen hái fābùle yīgè bāohán 1300 duō wàn GUI yuánsù de kuà píngtái shùjùjí, bìng zhèngmíngle OS-Atlas zài duōgè jīzhǔn cèshì zhōng de xiǎnzhù xiàonéng tǐshēng

1. This article discusses current efforts in building graphical user interface (GUI) agents.

2. Currently, these efforts primarily rely on powerful commercial vision language models (VLMs) such as GPT-4o and GeminiProVision.

3. Open-source VLMs perform poorly in GUI understanding and out-of-distribution (OOD) scenarios, making practitioners reluctant to use them.

4. To advance research in this field, the authors developed OS-Atlas, a foundational model adept at GUI understanding and OOD tasks.

5. They also released a cross-platform dataset containing over 13 million GUI elements and demonstrated significant performance improvements of OS-Atlas across multiple benchmark tests.
