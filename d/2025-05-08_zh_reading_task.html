1. Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) are rapidly advancing because they can understand and generate multiple modalities.

2. Previous models could only understand multiple modalities, but current models can generate and understand across different modalities.

3. Despite the existence of many benchmark tests, we cannot simply assume that high performance indicates stronger MLLM capabilities.

4. This project introduces General-Level, a framework for evaluating the performance and generality of MLLMs.

5. It assesses the progress of MLLMs by measuring the consistency of models in understanding and generation.
