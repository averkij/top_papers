Movie Gen: A Cast of Media Foundation Models

1. 这篇文章介绍了Movie Gen，一组能生成高质量1080p视频的基础模型。

2. 它可以进行精确的指令式视频编辑和基于用户图像的个性化视频生成。

3. 模型在多个任务上达到了最先进的水平，如文本到视频合成、视频个性化、视频编辑、视频到音频生成和文本到音频生成。

4. 最大的视频生成模型包含300亿参数，可生成长达16秒的视频。

5. 研究人员希望这项工作能加速媒体生成模型的进展和创新。

2. Tā kěyǐ jìnxíng jīngquè de zhǐlìngshì shìpín biānjí hé jīyú yònghù túxiàng de gèxìnghuà shìpín shēngchéng

3. Móxíng zài duō gè rènwù shàng dádàole zuì xiānjìn de shuǐpíng, rú wénběn dào shìpín héchéng, shìpín gèxìnghuà, shìpín biānjí, shìpín dào yīnyuè shēngchéng hé wénběn dào yīnyuè shēngchéng

4. Zuì dà de shìpín shēngchéng móxíng bāohán 300 yì cānshù, kě shēngchéng cháng dá 16 miǎo de shìpín

5. Yánjiū rényuán xīwàng zhè jiàn gōngzuò néng jiāsù méitǐ shēngchéng móxíng de jìnbù hé chuàngxīn

1. This article introduces Movie Gen, a set of foundational models capable of generating high-quality 1080p videos.

2. It can perform precise instructional video editing and personalized video generation based on user images.

3. The models have achieved state-of-the-art performance in multiple tasks, such as text-to-video synthesis, video personalization, video editing, video-to-audio generation, and text-to-audio generation.

4. The largest video generation model contains 30 billion parameters and can generate videos up to 16 seconds long.

5. The researchers hope that this work will accelerate the advancement and innovation of media generation models.
