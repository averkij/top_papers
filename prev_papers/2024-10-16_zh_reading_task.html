MLLM can see? Dynamic Correction Decoding for Hallucination Mitigation

1. 这篇文章讨论了多模态大语言模型（MLLMs）常见的幻觉现象及其原因。

2. 研究发现，虽然MLLMs在最终输出中错误生成对象，但它们在前面的层中实际上能够识别视觉对象。

3. 作者推测，这可能是由于语言模型的强知识先验抑制了视觉信息，导致幻觉。

4. 为此，作者提出了一种新的动态纠正解码方法（DeCo），能够适应性地选择合适的前面层并将知识整合到最终层以调整输出。

5. DeCo可以与各种经典解码策略无缝结合，并应用于不同的MLLMs。

6. 实验结果显示，DeCo能够显著降低幻觉率。

1. This article discusses the common phenomenon of hallucinations in multimodal large language models (MLLMs) and their causes.

2. Research has found that while MLLMs incorrectly generate objects in their final output, they are actually able to recognize visual objects in their earlier layers.

3. The authors speculate that this may be due to the strong knowledge priors of the language model suppressing visual information, leading to hallucinations.

4. To address this, the authors propose a new dynamic correction decoding method (DeCo), which can adaptively select appropriate earlier layers and integrate knowledge into the final layer to adjust the output.

5. DeCo can be seamlessly combined with various classical decoding strategies and applied to different MLLMs.

6. Experimental results show that DeCo can significantly reduce the hallucination rate.
