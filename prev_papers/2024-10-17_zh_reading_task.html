MixEval-X: Any-to-Any Evaluations from Real-World Data Mixtures

1. 这篇文章讨论了人工智能模型在处理多种模态信号时，评估标准不一致和存在偏差的问题。

2. 为解决这些问题，作者提出了MixEval-X，这是一个跨模态的实际应用评估基准。

3. MixEval-X通过混合和调整多模态基准，重建实际任务分布，确保评估结果与实际应用相符。

4. 研究表明，这种方法有效地使基准样本与实际任务分布一致，并且模型排名与众包实际评估结果高度相关。

5. 文章还提供了详细的排行榜，重新排列现有模型，并为未来研究提供了见解。

1. This article discusses the issues of inconsistent evaluation standards and biases in artificial intelligence models when handling multimodal signals.

2. To address these problems, the authors propose MixEval-X, a cross-modal practical application evaluation benchmark.

3. MixEval-X reconstructs the actual task distribution by mixing and adjusting multimodal benchmarks, ensuring that evaluation results align with real-world applications.

4. Research shows that this method effectively makes benchmark samples consistent with actual task distributions, and model rankings are highly correlated with crowdsourced actual evaluation results.

5. The article also provides a detailed leaderboard, reordering existing models and offering insights for future research.
