REINFORCE++: A Simple and Efficient Approach for Aligning Large Language Models

1. 这篇文章介绍了一种新的强化学习方法，叫做 REINFORCE++。

2. 它改进了经典的 REINFORCE 算法，结合了 PPO 的优化技术，但不需要评论网络。

3. REINFORCE++ 有三个主要目标：简单、提高训练稳定性和减少计算开销。

4. 实验结果显示，REINFORCE++ 比 GRPO 更稳定，比 PPO 更高效，性能也相当。

1. This article introduces a new reinforcement learning method called REINFORCE++.

2. It improves upon the classic REINFORCE algorithm by incorporating optimization techniques from PPO, without the need for a critic network.

3. REINFORCE++ has three main objectives: simplicity, enhanced training stability, and reduced computational overhead.

4. Experimental results show that REINFORCE++ is more stable than GRPO and more efficient than PPO, with comparable performance.
