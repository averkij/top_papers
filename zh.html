Bielik v3 Small: Technical Report

1. 我们介绍了 Bielik v3，这是一系列专为波兰语处理优化的参数高效生成文本模型（1.5B 和 4.5B）。

2. 这些模型展示了较小但优化良好的架构可以实现与大得多模型相当的性能，同时需要更少的计算资源。

3. 我们的方法包括自定义波兰语分词器（APT4）、平衡不同指令类型学习的加权指令交叉熵损失和根据训练进度动态调整的自适应学习率。

4. 经过精心策划的 292 亿个标记和 303 百万文档的训练，这些模型在多个基准测试中表现出色。

5. 4.5B 参数模型的结果与其 2-3 倍大小的模型竞争力相当，而 1.5B 模型则在其极其紧凑的配置下表现出强大的性能。

1. We introduced Bielik v3, a series of parameter-efficient text generation models optimized specifically for Polish language processing (1.

2. 5B and 4.

3. 5B).

4. These models demonstrate that smaller but well-optimized architectures can achieve performance comparable to much larger models while requiring fewer computational resources.

5. Our approach includes a custom Polish tokenizer (APT4), weighted instruction cross-entropy loss to balance learning across different instruction types, and adaptive learning rates dynamically adjusted according to training progress.

6. With meticulously planned training on 292 billion tokens and 303 million documents, these models perform excellently on multiple benchmarks.

7. The 4.

8. 5B parameter model's results are competitive with models 2-3 times its size, while the 1.

9. 5B model shows strong performance in its extremely compact configuration.
