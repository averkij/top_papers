1. This article discusses the limitations of natural language in handling multi-instance positional and attribute information.

2. To address this issue, the authors propose a diffusion model based on region instance control.

3. They introduce the ROI-Unpool operation, which, when combined with ROI-Align, achieves efficient and accurate regional operations.

4. Based on this technology, they present ROICtrl, an adapter that can precisely control regional instances.

5. Experiments show that ROICtrl performs exceptionally well in regional instance control and significantly reduces computational costs.
