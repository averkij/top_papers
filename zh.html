1. This article introduces an emerging technology: Interactive Generative Video (IGV).

2. It combines generative and interactive functions to produce high-quality video content and allows users to participate through control signals and response feedback.

3. IGV has important applications in three major areas: gaming, artificial intelligence, and autonomous driving.

4. The article also proposes five key modules for an ideal IGV system and analyzes the technical challenges and future directions for each module.
