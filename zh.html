rStar-Math: Small LLMs Can Master Math Reasoning with Self-Evolved Deep Thinking

1. 这篇文章介绍了 rStar-Math，展示了小型语言模型（SLMs）可以通过蒙特卡罗树搜索（MCTS）进行“深度思考”，从而媲美或超越 OpenAI o1 的数学推理能力。

2. rStar-Math 通过三项创新训练两个 SLMs：代码增强的思维链数据合成方法、更有效的过程偏好模型（PPM）训练方法和自我进化配方。

3. 经过四轮自我进化，rStar-Math 将 SLMs 的数学推理能力提升到最先进的水平。

1. This article introduces rStar-Math, demonstrating that Small Language Models (SLMs) can engage in "deep thinking" through Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS), thereby matching or surpassing the mathematical reasoning capabilities of OpenAI o1.

2. rStar-Math achieves this through three innovative approaches to train two SLMs: a code-enhanced chain-of-thought data synthesis method, a more effective Process Preference Model (PPM) training method, and a self-evolutionary recipe.

3. After four rounds of self-evolution, rStar-Math elevates the mathematical reasoning abilities of SLMs to the most advanced level.
