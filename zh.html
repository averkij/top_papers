ProcessBench: Identifying Process Errors in Mathematical Reasoning

1. 这篇文章讨论了语言模型在解决数学问题时常常出错的问题。

2. 它介绍了ProcessBench，一个用于测量识别数学推理错误步骤能力的工具。

3. ProcessBench包含3,400个测试案例，主要集中在竞赛和奥林匹克级别的数学问题。

4. 每个案例都有逐步解决方案和人类专家标注的错误位置。

5. 模型需要找出最早含有错误的步骤，或者得出所有步骤都正确的结论。

1. This article discusses the common issue of language models making errors when solving mathematical problems.

2. It introduces ProcessBench, a tool designed to measure the ability to identify steps where mathematical reasoning errors occur.

3. ProcessBench contains 3,400 test cases, primarily focused on competition and Olympiad-level mathematical problems.

4. Each case includes a step-by-step solution and error locations annotated by human experts.

5. The model is required to identify the earliest step containing an error or conclude that all steps are correct.
