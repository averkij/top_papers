MiniMax-Speech: Intrinsic Zero-Shot Text-to-Speech with a Learnable Speaker Encoder

1. We introduce MiniMax-Speech, a Transformer-based text-to-speech model capable of generating high-quality speech.

2. The key innovation of the model is a learnable speaker encoder that extracts voice characteristics from reference audio without the need for transcription.

3. This enables MiniMax-Speech to generate expressive and voice-consistent speech in zero-shot scenarios and supports one-shot voice cloning.

4. Through Flow-VAE, the overall quality of the synthesized audio is enhanced.

5. The model supports 32 languages and performs excellently across various evaluation metrics, achieving state-of-the-art results.
