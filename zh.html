1. This article discusses the research goal of creating artificial intelligence systems capable of long-term interaction with the environment.

2. Recent multimodal large language models (MLLMs) have made significant progress in understanding the open world but still face challenges in simultaneously handling perception, memory, and reasoning.

3. Existing MLLMs, due to their sequence-to-sequence architecture, cannot process inputs and generate responses concurrently.

4. To address this issue, the project introduces separate mechanisms for perception, reasoning, and memory, enabling real-time processing of video and audio inputs.

5. The proposed framework, IXC2.

6. 5-OL, includes a perception module for real-time processing of multimodal information, a memory module for integrating short-term and long-term memory, and a reasoning module for responding to queries and performing reasoning tasks.
