1. This article discusses how to improve multi-round image editing tasks.

2. Traditional methods are costly, and while large language models (LLMs) have planning capabilities, they lack accurate estimates of tool costs.

3. The authors propose the "CoSTA*" method, which combines LLMs and graph search to find cost-effective tool paths.

4. CoSTA* evaluates each subtask using a vision-language model (VLM), quickly recovers from failures, and switches modalities between subtasks to optimize cost and quality.

5. Experiments show that CoSTA* performs exceptionally well on a new multi-round image editing benchmark.
