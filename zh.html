ReasonMed: A 370K Multi-Agent Generated Dataset for Advancing Medical Reasoning

1. 这篇文章介绍了一个大型医学推理数据集ReasonMed。

2. 它结合详细的推理路径和简明的摘要，提高了医学问答模型的准确性。

3. 该数据集包含370k个高质量样本，通过多代理验证和改进过程生成。

4. 研究发现，结合详细的推理链和简明答案摘要的微调策略最有效。

5. 基于此策略训练的ReasonMed-7B模型，性能超越了之前的最佳模型。

1. Zhè piān wénzhāng jièshào le yīgè dàxíng yīxué tuílǐ shùjùjí ReasonMed

2. Tā jiēhé xiángxì de tuílǐ lùjìng hé jiǎnmíng de zhāiyào, tīgāo le yīxué wèndá móxíng de zhǔnquèxìng

3. Gǎi shùjùjí bāohán 370k gè gāo zhìliàng yàngběn, tōngguò duō dàilǐ yànzhèng hé gǎijìn guòchéng shēngchéng

4. Yánjiū fāxiàn, jiēhé xiángxì de tuílǐ liàn hé jiǎnmíng dá'àn zhāiyào de wēitiáo cèlüè zuì yǒuxiào

5. Jīyú cǐ cèlüè xùnliàn de ReasonMed-7B móxíng, xìngnéng chāoyuè le zhīqián de zuìjiā móxíng

1. This article introduces a large medical reasoning dataset called ReasonMed.

2. It combines detailed reasoning paths with concise summaries, enhancing the accuracy of medical question-answering models.

3. The dataset contains 370k high-quality samples, generated through a multi-agent validation and improvement process.

4. Research has found that the fine-tuning strategy combining detailed reasoning chains and concise answer summaries is the most effective.

5. The ReasonMed-7B model, trained using this strategy, outperforms previous best models.
