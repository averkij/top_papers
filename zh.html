BoostStep: Boosting mathematical capability of Large Language Models via improved single-step reasoning

1. 这篇文章讨论了大语言模型（LLMs）在解决复杂数学问题时的表现。

2. 模型使用分而治之的方法和上下文学习（ICL）示例。

3. 然而，ICL示例存在两个关键问题：粒度不匹配和负面效应噪声问题。

1. This article discusses the performance of large language models (LLMs) in solving complex mathematical problems.

2. The models use a divide-and-conquer approach and in-context learning (ICL) examples.

3. However, ICL examples face two key issues: granularity mismatch and negative impact noise problems.

4. These issues affect the quality of the model's reasoning.

5. The authors propose the BoostStep method, which aligns step granularity and provides relevant ICL examples through a "first attempt" strategy, thereby improving reasoning quality.

6. BoostStep can also be combined with the Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS) method to enhance model performance.

7. Experimental results show that BoostStep significantly improves the performance of GPT-4o and Qwen2.
