1. This article discusses the computational cost issue of super-resolution (SR) diffusion models.

2. Although some methods have accelerated SR models, they either lose authentic perceptual details or generate non-existent structures.

3. To address these problems, the authors propose RSD, a new distillation method.

4. RSD achieves single-step recovery by training a student network to produce images consistent with the teacher model and surpasses the teacher model.

5. Experimental results demonstrate that RSD performs excellently on multiple real and synthetic datasets.
