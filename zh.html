1. This article introduces a method called OmniConsistency.

2. It uses large-scale diffusion transformers to enhance style consistency and generalization ability in image-to-image pipelines without causing style degradation.

3. It achieves this goal through three contributions: a context consistency learning framework, a two-stage progressive learning strategy, and an interpolation design.

4. Experiments show that its performance approaches that of the top commercial model, GPT-4o.
