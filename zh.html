VideoRAG: Retrieval-Augmented Generation over Video Corpus

2. 它用来解决生成模型产生错误信息的问题。

4. 这种方法基于大型视频语言模型（LVLMs）来处理和表示视频内容。

1. 这篇文章介绍了一种叫做VideoRAG的新框架。 Zhè piān wénzhāng jièshào le yī zhǒng jiào zuò VideoRAG de xīn kuàngjià

2. 它用来解决生成模型产生错误信息的问题。 Tā yònglái jiějué shēngchéng móxíng chǎnshēng cuòwù xìnxī de wèntí

3. VideoRAG通过检索与查询相关的视频并利用视频的视觉和文本信息来生成输出。 VideoRAG tōngguò jiǎnsuǒ yǔ cháxún xiāngguān de shìpín bìng lìyòng shìpín de shìjué hé wénběn xìnxī lái shēngchéng shūchū

4. 这种方法基于大型视频语言模型（LVLMs）来处理和表示视频内容。 Zhè zhǒng fāngfǎ jīyú dàxíng shìpín yǔyán móxíng (LVLMs) lái chǔlǐ hé biǎoshì shìpín nèiróng

5. 实验证明，VideoRAG比其他相关方法更有效。 Shíyàn zhèngmíng, VideoRAG bǐ qítā xiāngguān fāngfǎ gèng yǒuxiào

1. This article introduces a new framework called VideoRAG.

2. It is designed to address the issue of generative models producing incorrect information.

3. VideoRAG works by retrieving videos relevant to a query and utilizing both the visual and textual information from the videos to generate outputs.

4. This method relies on large video-language models (LVLMs) to process and represent video content.

5. Experiments have shown that VideoRAG is more effective than other related methods.
